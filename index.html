Our Story

Red Lion Car Care was born from a passion for automotive excellence and a commitment to providing the highest quality detailing services. Founded by Nawaz, our journey began with a simple mission: to bring back the lion's shine in every vehicle we touch.

With years of experience and continuous learning, we've mastered the art of automotive detailing, ceramic coating, and paint protection. Our team uses only premium products from renowned brands like Labocosmetica and Opti-Coat to ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible.
